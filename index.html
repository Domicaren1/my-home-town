Top three activities to do in Owerri

Imo Palm Plantation

It is largest oil plantation in West Africa. It was setup in 1974.

Owerri Town

Welcome to the heart of Igboland, Owerri. Owerri (oh- WERR -ee,) is the capital of Imo State in Nigeria.

Ofe Owerri

Ofe Owerri literally means Owerri soup. It is the indigenous soup of the Owerri People.
